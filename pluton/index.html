CIRCUIT

The Circuit Of Audio

Circuit is a new brand, showcasing electronic music production. It is a platform to showcase the talents of unsigned scottish producers. Circuit realise that there is a lack of venues to play and we know that there is an overwhelming amount of talent throughout scotland that want a chance to perform.

We Circuit have experience in production, sound engineering, and sound design and we know just how hard it is to get your music out there. We are looking to bring culture to Edinburgh for the talented bedroom producer or electronic music artist. We understand that your platforms at the moment are simply online promotions through soundcloud or facebook. We understand that not all of you are DJ's or want to be DJ's. Most importantly we understand that you all need this platform.
